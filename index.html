  • Author: samuel Jenkins

    • okay, for the actual pizza review! Being a michiganer at heart, i have already tried hungy howies pizza, first of all i worked at one while i was in high school and it was the closest pizza place that delievered to my house in the boonies. first off, i would like to state that i was hesitant to order from a pizza joint like HH, due to the fact that i know what happens when pizzas are made... would rather have a clean metal machine make the pizza for me. The pepperoni pizza was overall good, 4.7/10. good for a fast order pizza. the cheese bread on the other hand was god awful. it was dry to eat, and was covered in butter, to the point where i have stains on my shirt now because of it. that i would score a 1.01/10 cant give full number scores as that is a rookie mistake, one i would not make! next on the review list is cottage inn. i have had many of their pizzas, but have never fully reviewed one.
